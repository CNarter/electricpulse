Our Story

Forged in the heart of Manchester's vibrant music scene, Electric Pulse was born from a shared love for raw, unfiltered rock and roll. From sweaty garage rehearsals to sold-out arenas, our journey has been a testament to the power of three chords and the truth.

We bring a sonic storm of thunderous drums, searing guitar solos, and vocals that cut straight to the soul. Our mission is simple: to create music that moves you, makes you feel alive, and leaves you buzzing long after the last note fades.
